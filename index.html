Assassin's Creed Odyssey was developed by Ubisoft Quebec. Development of the game commenced shortly following the release of Assassin's Creed Syndicate (2015).

Main Characters

Alexios, also known as Deimos, was a member of the Cult of Kosmos, a proto-Templar organization based in ancient Greece and a Tainted One.

Kassandra, also known as the Eagle Bearer or West Wind, was a Spartan mercenary who fought during the Peloponnesian War.
